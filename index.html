OUR MINISTRY

New Beginning was founded in 1994 by Pastors Charles and Ann
Leister. After pastoring on the road in Cleveland, Ohio and Plain
City, Ohio working to establish two new works, God placed within
Pastor Charles the vision for a new ministry in their home city of
Columbus, Ohio.

OUR PASTORS

Ann Leister gave her life to the
Lord in 1966 and was called to
the ministry in 1976. She
stands firm on the Word of God
and how He has delivered her
from sin. Preaching that Jesus
is still setting people free
today. Ann was ordained in
1982 and assigned to the
Evangelist Board of the
Church of the Living God.
